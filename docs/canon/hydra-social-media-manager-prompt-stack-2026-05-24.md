## Extracted Modes

The screenshots show eight practical social-content prompt modes.

### 1. Social Media Manager Mode

Function:

- Convert AI into a full-time social media manager for a chosen niche.
- Generate strategy across reels, static posts, hooks, CTAs, posting times, and engagement rules.

Hydra use:

- Campaign 001 planning.
- Daily Drop operations.
- Faction recruitment campaigns.
- AI talent account management.
- Shadow Moon content calendars.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Act as a social media strategy assistant for [niche]. Build a 30-day strategy with post types, hooks, CTAs, posting cadence, engagement tasks, and measurement checkpoints. Keep the plan realistic, platform-safe, and easy to execute.
```

### 2. Viral Content Factory

Function:

- Generate content ideas with hooks, storylines, captions, CTAs, and visual directions.
- Focus on retention, engagement, and shareability.

Hydra use:

- Infinite content idea generator.
- Prompt pack content modules.
- Character-led reel concepts.
- Shadow Arena faction posts.
- Hydra Visualiser scene cards.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Generate 20 high-potential content ideas for [niche]. For each idea include a hook, simple three-part storyline, caption angle, CTA, and visual concept. Prioritize clarity, usefulness, retention, and ethical engagement.
```

### 3. Caption & Copywriting Engine

Function:

- Produce high-engagement captions around a topic.
- Include hook, relatable context, emotional pull, and CTA.

Hydra use:

- Instagram captions.
- TikTok descriptions.
- Gumroad product blurbs.
- Discord announcements.
- Affiliate content captions.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Write 10 captions for [topic] in [brand voice]. Each caption should include a hook, relatable context, emotional pull, useful insight, and clear CTA. Avoid false claims or guaranteed outcomes.
```

### 4. 30-Day Posting Calendar Builder

Function:

- Build a complete month of posts for a niche.
- Include post type, caption hook, CTA, and suggested visuals.

Hydra use:

- Campaign calendar.
- Daily Drop scheduling.
- ManyChat keyword planning.
- Founder Pack execution guide.
- Content Console roadmap.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Create a 30-day posting calendar for [niche]. Include day, post type, content pillar, hook, caption summary, CTA, suggested visual, and engagement task. Format it as a clean execution schedule.
```

### 5. Competitor Analysis

Function:

- Analyze top competitors in a niche.
- Identify content patterns, tone, hooks, topics, and strategy gaps.

Hydra use:

- Market scan reports.
- Content positioning.
- Offer differentiation.
- Creator account benchmarking.
- Hydra Eyes intelligence briefs.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Analyze these competitors or examples: [paste links, names, or screenshots]. Identify their content patterns, tone, recurring hooks, strongest topics, audience promise, and opportunity gaps. Create a differentiated strategy for my brand.
```

Public-safe note:

- Use public data or user-provided screenshots.
- Do not scrape private accounts or misrepresent competitor performance.

### 6. Account Growth Accelerator

Function:

- Build a growth plan from current page link, stats, or audit notes.
- Include profile optimization, content themes, cadence, hashtags/keywords, and engagement workflows.

Hydra use:

- AI talent account audits.
- Creator brand launch plans.
- Agency client deliverables.
- Social Hydra Net dashboard recommendations.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Based on this page snapshot and stats [paste details], create a growth plan covering profile optimization, content themes, posting frequency, keyword/hashtag strategy, engagement workflows, and weekly metrics. Make it realistic and testable.
```

Public-safe note:

- Replace “guaranteed to work” framing with “testable,” “realistic,” and “measurable.”

### 7. Social Media Branding Architect

Function:

- Create a complete brand identity for social media.
- Include tone, color direction, messaging, content archetype, posting style, and audience personality.

Hydra use:

- Omega Hydra Talent Agency account setup.
- Character brand bibles.
- Faction page identity.
- Crown Obsidian Velvet design direction.
- Hydra Visualiser presets.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Design a social media brand identity for [niche or character]. Include tone of voice, visual direction, message pillars, content archetypes, posting style, audience personality, and examples of how the brand should sound.
```

### 8. Reel Script & Shot Guide

Function:

- Generate short-form video scripts with camera instructions, dialogue, scenes, transitions, and pacing notes.
- Optimize for watch-time and clarity.

Hydra use:

- TikTok / Reels production.
- Character recruitment clips.
- Campaign 001 faction videos.
- Hydra Visualiser scene packs.
- Shadow Moon micro-lore drops.

Safe prompt structure:

```text
Create 10 reel scripts for [niche]. For each script include hook, scene beats, camera direction, dialogue or voiceover, visual notes, transition idea, pacing guidance, CTA, and production difficulty.
```
