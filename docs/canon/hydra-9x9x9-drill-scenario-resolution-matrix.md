# III. The Nine Drill Families

| Drill | Drill Family | Core Focus |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Presence Drill** | posture, composure, attention, ethical starting state |
| 2 | **Perception Drill** | field reading, range recognition, hall recognition, contextual awareness |
| 3 | **Structure Drill** | alignment, Tri-Axis orientation, stability, organized movement |
| 4 | **Entry Drill** | purposeful transition, timing, range change, guided initiation |
| 5 | **Control Drill** | centerline clarity, directional organization, structured connection |
| 6 | **Protection Drill** | proportion, shielding, guardianship, supportive restraint |
| 7 | **Adaptation Drill** | changing rhythm, altered distance, branching choices, recovery |
| 8 | **Command Drill** | multi-system integration, instructor language, sequencing clarity |
| 9 | **Sovereignty Drill** | self-command, teaching transfer, reflection, stewardship |

# IV. The Nine Scenario Families

| Scenario | Scenario Family | Learning Condition |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Neutral Entry Scenario** | baseline orientation and starting clarity |
| 2 | **Range Shift Scenario** | changing distance, tempo, and access |
| 3 | **Hall Transition Scenario** | movement across center, cardinal, or diagonal halls |
| 4 | **Timing Disruption Scenario** | delayed cues, broken cadence, uncertain rhythm |
| 5 | **Multi-Variable Scenario** | several factors changing at once |
| 6 | **Protection Priority Scenario** | emphasis on safety, restraint, and guardianship |
| 7 | **Communication Pressure Scenario** | words, attention, and composure under strain |
| 8 | **Cognitive Load Scenario** | reduced clarity, need for disciplined simplification |
| 9 | **Command Integration Scenario** | multiple systems, teaching judgment, reflective clarity |

---

# V. The Nine Resolution Families

| Resolution | Outcome | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Recenter** | return to calm position and clear awareness |
| 2 | **Redirect** | alter path, angle, or attention to improve organization |
| 3 | **Stabilize** | regain structure and reduce disorder |
| 4 | **Contain** | organize pressure without unnecessary expansion |
| 5 | **Disengage** | create distance and close the learning loop safely |
| 6 | **Protect** | prioritize safeguarding of self, others, or space |
| 7 | **Adapt** | choose a new pathway when the first plan changes |
| 8 | **Command** | demonstrate integrated judgment and instructional clarity |
| 9 | **Reflect** | convert the event into learning, metrics, and refinement |
