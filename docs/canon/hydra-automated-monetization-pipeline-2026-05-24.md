## Canon Decision

This update canonizes the Hydra Automated Monetization Pipeline as the official revenue operating spine for Omega Hydra Talent Agency and the CEO Dashboard ecosystem.

The pipeline turns attention, prompts, lore, character assets, social content, community interactions, and product packs into a measurable business loop.

It is public-safe and business-focused. It must not use deceptive marketing, spam automation, fake engagement, misleading scarcity, or guaranteed-income claims.

## Revenue Spine

The canonical revenue spine remains:

```text
Package → Publish → Promote → Profit → Iterate
```

Expanded monetization loop:

1. **Package** — create prompt packs, icons, faction cards, codex PDFs, training modules, templates, dashboards, or community offers.
2. **Publish** — post on Instagram, TikTok, Threads, Discord, Gumroad, Whop, or the Hydra dashboard.
3. **Promote** — route attention through hooks, CTAs, ManyChat keywords, tracked links, affiliate links, and community events.
4. **Profit** — convert into free opt-ins, $9 entry products, $27 Founder Pack, $29 Foundership, affiliate commissions, or service calls.
5. **Iterate** — analyze Supabase events, content metrics, product conversions, feedback, and retention signals.

- Daily Drops.

- Instagram carousels.
- Character cards.
- Faction recruitment posts.
- Prompt-card screenshots.
- Robotics/AI trend commentary.

- Shadow Arena events.

Output:

### 2. Capture Layer

Primary mechanisms:

- ManyChat keyword comments.
- Link-in-bio pages.
- Gumroad free products.
- Whop free community access.
- Discord invite gates.
- Email capture pages.
- QR codes on prompt cards and PDFs.

```text

HYDRA
CODEX
PROMPT
FOUNDER

```

### 3. Routing Layer

Routing tools:

- Tracked redirect worker.
- Supabase `hydra_events` table.
- ManyChat webhook.
- Discord role assignment.
- Gumroad/Whop fulfillment links.
- Stripe checkout links.
- Affiliate SubID links.

Canonical event shape:

```json
{
  "event_type": "keyword_trigger | link_click | product_view | purchase | role_assigned | content_posted | community_joined",
  "source": "instagram | tiktok | threads | discord | gumroad | whop | email | affiliate",
  "campaign": "campaign_001_choose_your_crown",
  "sub_id": "post_or_offer_identifier",
  "user_ref": "privacy_safe_user_reference",
  "payload": {
    "offer": "starter_codex | prompt_pack | founder_pack | foundership",
    "faction": "crown | shadow | lotus | tiger | serpent",
    "cta": "string",
    "variant": "A | B | C"
  }
}
```

### 4. Offer Layer

### 7. Intelligence Layer

Hydra Eyes tracks:

- Content performance.
- Keyword conversion.
- Link click-through.
- Product view rate.
- Purchase conversion.
- Refund rate.
- Discord join rate.
- Role assignment success.
- Foundership retention.
- LTV by campaign and SubID.

### 8. Decision Layer

The CEO Dashboard uses Scale/Test/Kill decisions.

```text
Scale = strong signal, increase volume, make variants, route more traffic.
Test = unclear signal, change hook, offer, CTA, price, or audience.
Kill = weak signal, archive, repurpose, or stop promoting.
```

Decision thresholds should be configurable in the dashboard.

## Automation Roles: Hydra Triad Cell

- Validates numbers, flags anomalies, and prevents overreaction.

## Safety and Compliance Rules

Allowed:

- Ethical content marketing.
- Prompt packs.
- Digital downloads.
- Community membership.
- Transparent affiliate links.
- Tracked links with privacy-safe identifiers.
- Dashboard analytics.
- Public-safe fictional/cinematic branding.

Avoid:

- Fake engagement.
- Bot spam.
- Misleading scarcity.
- Guaranteed income claims.
- Unverified performance claims.
- Nonconsensual likeness content.
- Explicit adult-service promotion in public Hydra channels.
- Public tactical instruction from private professional material.
- Exposing service-role keys, private customer data, or sensitive logs.
