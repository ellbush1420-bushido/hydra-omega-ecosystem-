# IV. The Seven Source Pillars of Sonnal 88

## 1. Five-Range Hapkido
Provides the **range ladder** and transition logic.

### Five-range canon frame
1. **Outer Range** — awareness and pre-contact angle
2. **Bridge Range** — entry and interception
3. **Hand Range** — contact, redirection, and hand-line dominance
4. **Clinch Range** — balance disruption and containment
5. **Ground / Finish Range** — stabilization, disengagement, or controlled resolution

---

## 2. Iron Palm
Provides the **impact doctrine**:

- structural penetration symbolism
- compact force expression
- focused palm-line imagery
- “heavy hand, disciplined intent” identity

In Sonnal 88, Iron Palm is not treated as brute force alone, but as **condensed structural authority**.

---

## 3. Krav Maga
Provides the **urgency doctrine**:

- simplicity under stress
- rapid problem recognition
- direct path selection
- decisive exit-minded efficiency

Within Sonnal 88, this becomes the **Crisis Line**: when complexity rises, reduce motion to the clearest lawful objective.

---

## 4. Tai Sabaki
Provides the **angle doctrine**:

- off-line movement
- body displacement
- entry by geometry rather than collision
- transition through diagonal and circular routes

Tai Sabaki becomes the **Shadow Pivot** branch of Sonnal 88.

---

## 5. 3×3 Lo Shu Grid
Provides the **spatial doctrine**:

- nine-position hall logic
- centerline authority
- diagonal influence
- tactical map visualization
- scenario placement within the Hydra training language

The Lo Shu Grid gives Sonnal 88 its **9-Hall movement grammar**.

---

## 6. Tri-Axis
Provides the **dimensional doctrine**:

- **Vertical Axis** — rise, drop, rooting, elevation
- **Horizontal Axis** — angle, lateral drift, flank, breadth
- **Depth Axis** — enter, withdraw, compress, extend

Tri-Axis keeps Sonnal 88 from becoming a flat striking system; it makes it a **three-dimensional range and body-orientation engine**.

---

## 7. Geumgang Yuksa
Provides the **guardian-archetype doctrine**:

- diamond firmness
- threshold guardianship
- sacred restraint
- protective force posture

In Hydra canon, Geumgang Yuksa is the **mythic body of the system**: the order’s guardian-force image for Sonnal 88.
