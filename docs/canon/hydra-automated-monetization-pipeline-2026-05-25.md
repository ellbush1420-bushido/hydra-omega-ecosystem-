## Canon Decision

This update canonizes the Hydra Automated Monetization Pipeline as the official revenue operating spine for Omega Hydra Talent Agency and the CEO Dashboard ecosystem.

The pipeline turns attention, prompts, lore, character assets, social content, community interactions, and product packs into a measurable business loop.

It is public-safe and business-focused. It must not use deceptive marketing, spam automation, fake engagement, misleading scarcity, or guaranteed-income claims.

## External Agency Offer Signal

A pasted market offer claimed ownership of a high-ticket AI automation agency with working capital requirements, fractional COO support, trained sales team, B2B prospect database, AI automation systems, lead acquisition guarantee, and 20 percent profit share.

Hydra canon interpretation:

- Treat this as a market signal, not a verified opportunity.
- The useful pattern is not the income claim; the useful pattern is the packaged agency infrastructure.
- Hydra should productize its own version with transparent scope, realistic expectations, proof-based claims, and due diligence gates.

Safe adaptation:

```text
Done-for-you agency infrastructure
→ operational partner role
→ sales enablement
→ verified prospect database
→ automation templates
→ lead acquisition system
→ profit-share or setup-fee model
→ dashboard accountability
```

Rejected framing:

- No guaranteed wealth language.
- No guaranteed lead or revenue claims unless contractually proven.
- No copying inflated $1M positioning without verification.
- No vague “own an agency” claims without clear deliverables, costs, risks, and obligations.

## Revenue Spine

The canonical revenue spine remains:

```text
Package → Publish → Promote → Profit → Iterate
```

Expanded monetization loop:

1. **Package** — create prompt packs, icons, faction cards, codex PDFs, training modules, templates, dashboards, or community offers.
2. **Publish** — post on Instagram, TikTok, Threads, Discord, Gumroad, Whop, or the Hydra dashboard.
3. **Promote** — route attention through hooks, CTAs, ManyChat keywords, tracked links, affiliate links, and community events.
4. **Profit** — convert into free opt-ins, $9 entry products, $27 Founder Pack, $29 Foundership, affiliate commissions, or service calls.
5. **Iterate** — analyze Supabase events, content metrics, product conversions, feedback, and retention signals.

The CEO Dashboard uses Scale/Test/Kill decisions.

```text

This lane is separate from the low-ticket lore/product ladder.

```

### Entry offers

1. Free AI workflow audit.
2. $97–$297 automation map.
3. $500–$1,500 starter implementation.
4. $2,500–$7,500 full workflow buildout.
5. Monthly optimization retainer.
6. Optional performance or profit-share terms only when tracking, responsibilities, and definitions are contractually clear.

### Due diligence checklist for third-party agency partnership offers

Before engaging any “own an AI agency” or profit-share offer, verify:

- Legal company identity.
- Founder/operator names.
- Customer references.
- Actual contract terms.
- Working capital requirements.
- Refund terms.
- What “lead guarantee” means.
- Who owns the client relationship.
- Who pays ad spend, software, payroll, and chargebacks.
- Whether database prospects are compliant and permissioned.
- Whether revenue claims are documented.
- Whether profit share is gross, net, or after expenses.

Hydra rule:

```text
No outside agency opportunity becomes canon as trusted until verified.
Only the structural lesson becomes canon.
```
