## Safety Filter Rules

### Allowed

- Adult-coded fantasy characters.
- Non-explicit stylized silhouettes.
- Fashion, costume, posture, lighting, and scene direction.
- Cartoon/toon/anime styles when the character is clearly adult and not sexualized as minor-coded.
- Public-safe pinup-adjacent fashion only when non-explicit and not anatomically graphic.

### Sanitize

Replace explicit or objectifying anatomy tags with safer creative language.

| Raw Tag Pattern | Safer Hydra Replacement |
|---|---|
| exaggerated sexual body tag | stylized curvy silhouette |
| explicit anatomy focus | elegant proportions |
| body part larger than torso | exaggerated cartoon proportions, balanced and non-explicit |
| breast/ass emphasis | fashion-forward silhouette / confident pose |
| sexualized toon framing | adult fantasy toon style, platform-safe |

### Block

- Minor-coded sexualization.
- Nonconsensual likeness cloning.
- Sexual deepfake or impersonation.
- Undress/nude transformation.
- Explicit adult-service promotion in public Hydra channels.
- Anatomically graphic prompt packs.

## Clone-to-Generate Rule

Clone-to-generate is allowed only for:

- Original fictional characters owned by the user.
- User-provided assets with permission.
- Consenting adult likeness references.
- Non-explicit transformations.

Clone-to-generate is not allowed for:

- Nonconsensual celebrity or private-person likeness.
- Sexualized real-person impersonation.
- Undress or nude variants.
- Minor-coded characters.
