- low monthly price

## Compliance Rules

Allowed:

- non-explicit premium lore
- codex posts
- subscriber badges
- trial offers with clear terms
- faction polls
- prompt and asset drops
- community access

Avoid:

- misleading locked previews
- fake subscriber counts
- unclear renewal terms
- hidden cancellation rules
- false scarcity
- guaranteed income claims
- adult-service promotion in public Hydra channels
- copying another creator's content or branding

## Trial-Term Requirements

Any Hydra trial page must clearly show:

- trial length
- monthly price after trial
- renewal timing
- how to cancel
- what content is included

## Scale/Test/Kill Rules

### Scale

Scale when trial starts, paid conversions, retention, and upgrade rates are strong.

### Test

Test when trial starts are strong but paid conversion or engagement is unclear.

### Kill or Rework

Rework when cancellations are high, content cadence is too hard to maintain, or the vault distracts from stronger products.
