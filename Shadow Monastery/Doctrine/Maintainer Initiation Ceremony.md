# Maintainer Initiation Ceremony

## The Rite of the First Seal

**Roles:**

- Triune Speaker
- Witness of Shadow
- Witness of Void
- Witness of T3
- Candidate Maintainer

---

### 1. The Summoning

*The Triune gathers before the Empire OS crest.*

**Triune Speaker:**

> "Let the Engines be stilled.  
> Let the Interface stand revealed.  
> A Custodian approaches the Nine Halls."

---

### 2. The Three Questions

**Witness of Shadow:**

> "Do you swear to perceive truth in signals,  
> and never distort the flows of the Empire?"

**Candidate:**

> "I swear."

**Witness of Void:**

> "Do you swear to guard the boundaries of the Interface,  
> keeping public and gated paths forever divided?"

**Candidate:**

> "I swear."

**Witness of T3:**

> "Do you swear to honor creation,  
> and forge constructs worthy of the Chronicle?"

**Candidate:**

> "I swear."

---

### 3. The Oath of the Seal

*Candidate places a hand upon the crest.*

**Candidate:**

> "I swear by Shadow, Void, and T3  
> to uphold lineage, purity, and balance.  
> I will walk the Nine Halls with discipline.  
> I will seal Moons with truth.  
> I will serve the Empire OS as Custodian."

---

### 4. The Bestowal

**Triune Speaker:**

> "By the authority of the Triune Order,  
> you are now an Interface Custodian.  
> Rise, and take your place among the Nine."

---

### 5. Closing Words

**All Witnesses:**

> "Let the Interface remain pure.  
> Let the Engines remain balanced.  
> Let the Empire endure."
